Welcome to Shushani Winery!


A boutique winery in Safed, offering unique homemade wine.


At Shushani Winery, we are proud to present our exceptional wine, crafted with love and tradition in the magical city of Safed.


Shushani wine is made from high-quality hand-picked grapes, with meticulous attention to every step of the production process, from the vineyard to the bottle. We employ ancient traditional methods, combined with new knowledge and technology, to create a wine with a rich and refreshing taste.


Shushani wine is perfect for any occasion and any celebration. It has a delicate fruity taste with aromas of roses and citrus. It is excellent for drinking on its own or as a perfect accompaniment to a light meal or cheeses.


We invite you to visit our winery and experience our unique winemaking process up close. You can tour the vineyards, learn about the production process, and even taste our exquisite wines.
